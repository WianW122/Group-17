How It Works

1. Donations Logged

Donor stores register sports equipment donations. Items are tracked and stored digitally.

2. Pickups Scheduled

Once ready, logistics teams plan collection routes and move items to distribution centers.

3. Equipment Delivered

NPOs receive gear and confirm delivery, ensuring transparency and impact tracking.
